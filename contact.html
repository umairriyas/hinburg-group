FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from businesses exploring operational support and infrastructure coordination services with Hinborg Infrastructure Group.

Who will I be working with directly?
You will work directly with John Stanley Hinborg — the owner — on every aspect of your engagement. There are no account managers, junior consultants or handoffs. John personally handles all client communication, project planning and service delivery from start to finish.
What types of businesses do you typically work with?
We primarily serve engineering firms, construction companies, facilities management businesses, infrastructure-focused organizations and businesses operating across multiple locations. If your business operates in a complex, coordination-heavy environment — we are likely a strong fit.
Do you work with businesses outside of Missouri?
Yes. While we are based in Waverly, Missouri, Hinborg Infrastructure Group supports clients across multiple states. Many of our services — including documentation management, workflow design and systems reliability — can be delivered remotely or through structured site visits as required.
How quickly can you start supporting our operations?
After an initial consultation and scope alignment, most engagements can begin within one to two weeks. For urgent operational needs, we can often move faster — contact us directly to discuss your timeline and we will do our best to accommodate your schedule.
Can you support both short-term projects and long-term engagements?
Absolutely. We offer both project-based engagements for specific, time-bound needs and ongoing operational support partnerships for businesses that need consistent, long-term coordination and systems management. We will recommend the model that best fits your situation.
How do I get started?
Simply fill in the inquiry form above or send an email directly to john@hinborginfrastructuregroup.com. John will personally review your inquiry and respond within one business day to schedule an initial consultation at no obligation.
