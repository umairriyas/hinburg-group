Who We Are

Owner-Led. Precision-Driven. Built to Last.

Hinborg Infrastructure Group is an owner-led engineering and infrastructure support firm based at 108 West Elm Street in Waverly, Missouri. Founded and personally led by John Stanley Hinborg, the firm brings structured coordination, operational clarity and systems-focused thinking to engineering, construction and facilities businesses where precision and reliability are non-negotiable.

Unlike large consultancy firms with multiple layers of management, Hinborg Infrastructure Group operates with a direct, hands-on model. Every engagement is managed personally — meaning clients receive experienced, consistent leadership from the very first conversation through to full project delivery. There are no handoffs, no miscommunication and no loss of context.

With over 15 years of experience across engineering environments, construction operations, facilities management and multi-location businesses, Hinborg Infrastructure Group has built a reputation for delivering structured, reliable and practical operational support that genuinely moves projects forward.
